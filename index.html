What is our study about?

Our research aims to assess the effectiveness of curriculum automatically generated by a generative model in enhancing data visualization literacy. You will be presented with various chart types and asked to list all the topics as if you were an experienced data visualization instructor guiding novice students. The prototype will be accessible online, requiring no installation.
